About me

My name is Samuel Rios.

I am a video game programmer specializing in networking for online multiplayer games, with experience on client-server and shard based architectures.

Skills

My best skill is, no doubt, programming on C# using Unity engine.

But C# is not my unique language. I use other languages as Java or C++, and scripting languages as Javascript.

Other video game engines I have used are: Unreal Engine using blueprints, Phaser3 framework and Game Maker Studio.

I work daily with relational databases, such as ORACLE SQL and POSTGRE SQL. I have also used SQLITE and non-relational databases as MongoDB.

I am quite experienced in handling thread concurrency for servers, and using low and high level TCP sockets. My latest project is mainly focused on making a base server for video games, as a Unity package. I'll talk more about it soon, if you are interested you can see the code here.

In my working experience as IT Manager, I have been constantly confronted with the use of Spring servers with Maven in Java, API-REST calls and their endpoints, and API Manager (WSO2). Some older technologies such as JSP, Servlet or SOAP are also included. As for other tools, I have knowledge in continuous integration with Jenkins and task management using JIRA.

To end this summary, I manage the Git version control system (GitHub and Bitbucket), office tools as spreadsheets or word processors, and I have some knowledge about computer security (6th place at INCIBE Cyberolympics 2016).

Marshallow: Pilferage in YolkTown

Marshallow: Pilferage in YolkTown is a single-player 3D video game set in a town in festivities. Among all the villagers a thief is hiding and your duty as a marshal is to find him and catch him. To do this you must talk to the villagers who have been victims or witnesses of the thief's thefts, who will give you details of the thief's appearance. But be careful, you should watch out with the villagers, as they may share characteristics or accessories with the thief and some of them will give you doubtful information.

Astral Knock Out

Astral Knock Out is an online multiplayer fighting and platform game that is set in an environment that mixes outer space and fantasy. A game consists of 1vs1 fights between warriors from different planets formed in the different arts of the ranged combat that fight for defeating their opponent and becoming the champion.

21 Singularity

21 Singularity is an asymmetric local multiplayer game for 3 players with side view, in which 2 of the players take the role of androids who must cooperate to get out of the factory, while the third player will be the evil scientist who will try to prevent it.

Dance Kingdom

Dance Kingdom is a musical strategy game where you will have to follow the rhythm to obtain gold that will allow you to form and send troops to the battlefield.

The Bat Mafia

As a participation for the #KenneyJam2020, with theme "Cursed", a partner and I decided to make "The Bat Mafia". An top-down view arcade video game in which you carry a baseball bat which will try to kill the people around you. You must advance by taking out the bat mafia, but avoid the citizens!

Battle of Villages

Battle of Villages is a 1v1 online multiplayer card game for mobile phones in which three opposing villages (Karma, Thunder and Vital Energy) fight for the sacred territory between them.
