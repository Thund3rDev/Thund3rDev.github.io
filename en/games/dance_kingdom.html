Dance Kingdom

Dance Kingdom is a musical strategy game where you will have to follow the rhythm to obtain gold that will allow you to form and send troops to the battlefield.

It was a university project, in this case of 3 people for the subject "Human Machine Interaction and Usability", where we were asked to make a game taking into account accessibility principles to make it playable for as many people as possible, and also usability principles to facilitate and accommodate the user experience.

The technology used in the project is Unity.

In this project, I worked with 2 artists, so all the programming is done by me.

As an interesting fact, the project was worked on and realized with a dancing blanket as a controller (the game read inputs from the dancing blanket for its functioning), but so that it could be played by more people, we decided to publish it as a keyboard game. Although you can always do a local remapping!
