Battle Of Villages

Battle of Villages is a 1v1 online multiplayer card game for mobile phones in which three opposing villages (Karma, Thunder and Vital Energy) fight for the sacred territory between them.

This is my personal project, it is not finished yet, in fact it is in a very early stage of development, but I want to do it little by little.

In Battle of Villages the combats are automatically performed from left to right, so that players only have to play the card they want to call to battle, simultaneously. This is one of the principles on which the game is based, since the idea is to make it simple and accessible to everyone, so that it can appeal to both novice and experienced players in the card genre.
