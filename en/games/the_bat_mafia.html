The Bat Mafia

As a participation for the #KenneyJam2020, with theme "Cursed", a partner and I decided to make "The Bat Mafia". An top-down view arcade video game in which you carry a baseball bat which will try to kill the people around you. You must advance by taking out the bat mafia, but avoid the citizens!

The technology used in the project is Unity.

The Game Jam was only 48 hours long, so we divided the work as best we could, and my parts were as follows:

  • Design and implementation of the map using tiles.
  • Playable character controller.
  • Diseño de nivel (posición de enemigos, aliados y obstáculos).
  • Sound implementation.
  • Lighting.
  • Post-processing.
  • Testing.
